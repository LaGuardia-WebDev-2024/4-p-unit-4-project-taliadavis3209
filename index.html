My Unofficial Very Official Local Guide to New York City

Why Should YOU go to New York

  • Arts and culture
  • Broadway: Catch a world-renowned theatrical performance.
  • Museums: Explore collections at the Metropolitan Museum of Art, the American Museum of Natural History, the Guggenheim, or the Museum of Modern Art.
  • Art Galleries: Discover both established and emerging artists in numerous galleries. The High Line: Walk along this unique elevated park built on a historic freight rail line.
  • Food and shopping
  • World-class restaurants: Enjoy a vast and diverse culinary scene with everything from street food to fine dining.
  • Shopping: Shop your heart out along Fifth Avenue or discover unique boutiques in different neighborhoods.
  • Ethnic neighborhoods: Explore areas like Little Italy or Chinatown for unique food and experiences.
  • Unique experiences
  • Diverse neighborhoods: Discover the unique character of different areas, such as Greenwich Village, Harlem, or Williamsburg.
  • Grand Central Terminal: Marvel at the architecture of this historic transportation hub.
  • Bryant Park: Enjoy this beautiful public space, home to the New York Public Library.

The Eiffle Tower!!!

You should visit the Eiffel Tower for its iconic status, the stunning panoramic views of New York from its upper levels, and the magical experience of seeing it illuminated at night. Visitors can also enjoy various amenities, such as dining at restaurants or exploring interactive displays on the lower levels.



Leaning Tower of Pisa

People visit the Leaning Tower of Pisa primarily for its iconic, unintended tilt, which makes it a world-famous architectural marvel and a popular tourist attraction. Visitors also come to experience its beauty, its history as part of a UNESCO World Heritage site, and the opportunity to climb to the top for unique views of the city.
